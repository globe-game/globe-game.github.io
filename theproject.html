What is G.L.O.B.E.?

Yeah, that's right!


The game

G.L.O.B.E. is a fun family racing game, like 'Mario Kart'.

Compete against artificial intelligence or your friends in a variety of circuits, with vehicles each crazier than the others!

Travel around Astra and try to make a reputation for yourself on the circuit, and finally become the number 1!


The story

Planet Astra, 900 years in the future. Human beings have been forced to leave the Earth due to a sudden acceleration of global warming.

Despite this, and while trying to limit the pollution of their new habitat, humans have not given up the speed of their vehicles.

G.L.O.B.E. is a worldwide competition bringing together the most powerful vehicles on the planet.

Competitions bring in a lot of money, both for the participants and the organizers.

The races take place in the four corners of this globe 2.0.


The vehicles

The technology has evolved, but don't worry, most of the vehicles will still be very familiar to you!

You will start your adventure with a charming low poly car. Its name is Espada.

With the money you earn from winning races, you will be able to purchase new vehicles.

And for the occasion, the inhabitants of Astra knew how to show originality!

It goes from the simple go-kart to the futuristic car, through... a 'grand bi'???

Gotta catch 'em all! Uh, not the right game. Gotta BUY them all, and choose the one that will take you straight to victory!



Yes, it was not a joke, here's a 'grand bi'. Oh and this is Espada, with which you will start your adventure. And finally, Chouf, the truck with the greatest pride in the world.

Artificial intelligence (single player mode)

You really want to play but you don't have any friends? No problem!

Play solo against the artificial intelligence.

The AI is designed to dodge obstacles in an optimal way.

The AI follows a predefined path, so don't waste time in the background, the AI won't wait for you!


Multiplayer mode

Did you manage to convince your friends to play G.L.O.B.E.? Well done, I didn't succeed myself.

The game has a "room" system. A player creates a room, and all other players can see it and join it.

There you are! Go ahead, and may the best player win!
